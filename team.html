One of the biggest problems the world is confronting nowadays is the huge amount of pollution produced by the awful management of the energy in people's homes. Azalea is a project, designed by the Solar Decathlon UPV group, whose main objective is to give a solution to this issue, by creating a passive and sustainable house. It also pretends to rais awareness.
